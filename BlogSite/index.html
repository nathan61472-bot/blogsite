The About Menu

Hi! I’m glad you’re here. My name is Andre Jesus D. Doriano, and I am a 4th-year tourism management student at Saint Louis University in Baguio City. I am a travel and lifestyle blogger who was born in the beautiful city of Baguio on July 1, 2004, and now lives in Urdaneta City. Growing up in two vibrant places allowed me to appreciate nature, people, and the simple moments that make life worth exploring. I’ve always believed that every journey, short or long, has a story, and through this blog, I want to share mine.

Ever since I learned how to drive, I discovered my love for traveling to nature spots, beach escapes, mountain views, and breathtaking sunsets. There’s something magical about being on the road, feeling the wind, and watching the world unfold one destination at a time. When I’m not exploring, you can probably find me playing basketball or enjoying a quiet game of billiards with friends. These hobbies keep me grounded and remind me of the balance between adventure and everyday life.

But this blog isn’t just about where I go; it’s about why I go. It’s about finding peace in sunsets, joy in road trips, and meaning in discovering new places. It’s about appreciating life, collecting memories, and growing through experiences. When life is difficult and when everything turns bad, always remember that even a turtle can finish a race as long as he never gives up.
